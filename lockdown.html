During those days, the amount of activities dramatically decreased because of the quarantine. COVID- 19 disease (coronavirus) is modifying our lives radically: “We all want things to go back to normal quickly. But what most of us have probably not yet realized—yet will soon—is that things won’t go back to normal after a few weeks, or even a few months. Some things never will.” (Gideon Lichfield, director of Mit Technology Review). I decided to document the events of a family in Milan, where quarantine has been keeping people inside their houses to confine the infection.
