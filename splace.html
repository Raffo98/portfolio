Splace is the first service for smart workers aimed at the development of micro-communities through partnerships with local structures and private individuals. It is born to overcome the monotony of the workersâ€™ life from home, allowing them to change workplace when they want. After taking care of the entire communication by developing the name, logo, visual identity and tone of voice, we defined several touchpoints with which the user can interact. The smartphone application is the heart of the experience, it allows the users to explore places near them frequented by other workers with similar interests. He can also create or join groups and participate in events organized by the hosts of the premises. In addition to this there is the website, a web app for tablets, an instagram page and a wireless charger present on the tables of the affiliated premises.
